# CropClient + ClientAI System Overview

## Purpose

CropManage is the online irrigation management system used by California growers to plan, track, and report agricultural water use.

CropClient is the interaction and automation layer that surfaces this data and enables mobile, web, and AI-assisted workflows.

ClientAI is the controlled AI layer that connects natural language requests to approved CropClient tools (MCP).

---

## Why CropManage Exists

CropManage provides growers and managers with:

- scientifically based irrigation recommendations  
- historical irrigation tracking  
- water budget management  
- compliance reporting for California requirements  
- a single system of record for irrigation activity  

**System of Record Rule:**  
All irrigation truth lives in CropManage data (via CRUD and grids).
